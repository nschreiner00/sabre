Competitive Napping

Two teams, one judge, and ten naps!

Competitive napping is when two teams have a 'siesta match' to determine the best group of nappers.

The winner is chosen by whichever team has the most points.

To determine the merits of a nap, the total score is based on:

  • Speed of repose
  • Sleep posture
  • Slumber depth
  • Time to achieve wakefulness
