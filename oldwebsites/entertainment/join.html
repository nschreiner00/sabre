Napping with friends!

Club participation is a way to plug into the university in a way that allows you to connect with others and to make a positive impact on the university and greater community.  Clubs can help you form relationships with peers who share your interests, give you an opportunity to become a leader or contribute as a team member, raise awareness in the community, and make a lasting impact on campus. Club participation can be a great way to translate what you have learned in the classroom into reality, or can provide an outlet from the stress of academics by providing a creative outlet.

Sticking with the same club for multiple years can provide a rewarding experience as you watch the club and its members grow, and help shape the organization with your individual vision.
